THOUGHTS ON
The Sky Kitchen celebrates European Cuisine through traditional techniques, the finest ingredients and contemporary flair. Each kitchen is led by a
talented chef whose passion lies in creating uniquely rooted yet modern dishes.
With international outposts in some of the world’s most vibrant cities, including Pune, The Sky Kitchen menu is a culinary balance of signature dishes
available at The Sky Kitchen location and dishes curated with local tastes and traditions.
For exclusive private events or large group bookings, The Sky Kitchen Pune has a selection of areas and menus to choose from.

Our Team
Our team – cooks or servers, butchers or bartenders – speak to any of them and you’ll realize one thing: what ties us all together is that we all want
to put a smile on the face of every guest that walks through the doors of The Sky Kitchen. We want you to come in and forget what you’ve been through,
because we’re here to take care of you!

But as we do that we also take care of each other, learn from each other, grow as a team and most importantly have fun while doing all of this.
The Sky Kitchen, one of Pune's most celebrated restaurants, has welcomed guests to enjoy its globally inspired cuisine and hospitality.
Located in the iconic area of Pune, from introducing the concept of small and large plates to providing a true farm-to-table experience,
The Sky Kitchen continues to be at the forefront of Indian hospitality.
